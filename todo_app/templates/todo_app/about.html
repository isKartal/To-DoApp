TO-DO LIST

In our daily lives, managing tasks, staying productive, and maintaining effective time management require a powerful planning tool. This is where the To-Do List application comes into play!

This application provides a simple yet powerful platform where users can easily add, edit, and mark completed tasks. Designed with a user-friendly interface, it offers an effective task management system that helps you stay organized and in control of your workload.

Key Features:

Add Tasks: Easily add new tasks to your daily to-do list.

Update and Edit Tasks: Modify or update existing tasks as needed.

Mark Tasks as Complete: Mark tasks as completed and remove them from the list.

User Login: Manage your personal tasks by logging into your account.

Easy to Use: With its simple and intuitive design, it’s accessible for everyone.

With this application, you can easily manage your to-do list and use your time more efficiently. It’s the perfect tool to organize your daily tasks and stay productive.
